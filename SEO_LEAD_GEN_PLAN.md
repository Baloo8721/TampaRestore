# TampaRestore SEO & Lead Generation Plan

**Last Updated:** May 2026  
**Owner:** Tyler B.

## Executive Summary

The water damage restoration market runs on **emergency intent** — when someone searches, they need help NOW. Your strategy captures that moment in two places: **Google Maps (Map Pack)** and **organic search results**.

The single most valuable asset is your **Google Business Profile (GBP)**, not your website. Most homeowners call directly from the Map Pack without visiting a website.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1–2)

Complete all of these before moving forward.

### 1.1 Claim & Optimize Google Business Profile

Your GBP is your primary lead generation tool.

**Action Items:**

1. Go to business.google.com and search for "TampaRestore" — claim or create listing
2. Set business name exactly as: TampaRestore
3. **Primary category:** Water Damage Restoration Service
4. **Secondary categories:** Mold Removal Service, Fire Damage Restoration Service
5. **Address:** Hide from customers (serve at customer location)
6. **Service areas:** Tampa, Pasco County, Hernando County, Hillsborough County
7. **Phone:** Use your Google Voice number
8. **Hours:** Open 24 hours
9. **Description (750 characters max):**
   - Include: water damage restoration, emergency water extraction, structural drying, Tampa, Pasco County, IICRC certified, insurance accepted
   - Example: "24/7 emergency water damage restoration serving Tampa Bay and Pasco County. IICRC certified technicians. We work directly with all insurance providers. Fast response — call now for immediate help."
10. **Photos:** Upload team, equipment, truck, before/after project photos (weekly)

### 1.2 Ensure NAP Consistency

Name, Address, and Phone must be identical everywhere.

**Action:**

1. Write your exact business info:
   - Business name: ________
   - Address: ________
   - Phone: ________
   - Format: (XXX) XXX-XXXX (pick one format)
2. Search your business name online
3. Correct any inconsistencies on: Yelp, HomeAdvisor, Angie List, BBB, Yellow Pages, local chamber

### 1.3 Configure Formspree

1. Go to formspree.io — create free account
2. Create new form — copy the form ID
3. Replace YOUR_FORM_ID on line 1133 in index.html

### 1.4 Add Your Phone Number

1. Get Google Voice number (voice.google.com) — free, 813 or 727 area code
2. Forward to your contractor partner's phone
3. Replace (YOURNUMBER) in index.html (lines 782, 801, 862, 1085, 1104)
4. Add LLC name to lines 1093 and 1106

## Phase 2: Website SEO Optimization (Week 2–4)

### 2.1 Create Individual Service Pages

Each core service needs its own dedicated page.

**New Pages to Create:**

| Page | Target Keyword |
|------|---------------|
| /water-damage-restoration.html | water damage restoration Tampa |
| /flood-cleanup.html | flood cleanup Tampa |
| /mold-remediation.html | mold removal Tampa |
| /sewage-cleanup.html | sewage backup cleanup Tampa |
| /storm-damage-restoration.html | storm damage restoration Tampa |

**Each Page Must Include:**

- H1: "24/7 [Service] in Tampa"
- 2–3 paragraphs of unique, conversational content
- FAQ section (5–8 questions homeowners ask)
- Click-to-call button in the hero
- Internal link back to main site

### 2.2 Create Location Pages

Each city needs its own page with genuine local content. Thin doorway pages get penalized — each page needs real content.

**Priority Cities:**

| Page | Include |
|------|---------|
| /tampa.html | Neighborhoods served, local testimonials |
| /wesley-chapel.html | Pasco County references |
| /new-port-richey.html | Local landmarks |
| /zephyrhills.html | Community references |

**Content Requirements per Page:**

- Unique intro paragraph mentioning the city
- 3–5 local neighborhoods or landmarks
- At least one testimonial from that city
- Service area map or list (not keyword stuffed)
- FAQs specific to that area
- Internal links to service pages

### 2.3 Add Schema Markup

Add LocalBusiness schema to help Google understand your business.

### 2.4 Technical SEO Checklist

- [ ] Mobile loads under 3 seconds — test with Google PageSpeed Insights
- [ ] All images have alt text
- [ ] H1 appears on each page (only one per page)
- [ ] Meta description on every page (under 160 characters)
- [ ] Internal links between pages

## Phase 3: Content Strategy (Ongoing)

### 3.1 Blog Topics That Drive Leads

Write content answering questions homeowners ask in the first 24 hours:

- "What to do when a pipe bursts"
- "Will insurance cover water damage?"
- "How long does drying take?"
- "What causes mold after flooding?"
- "How much does water damage restoration cost in Tampa?"

Each blog post: 800–1200 words with local Tampa angle.

### 3.2 FAQ Content

Add expanded FAQ to main page:

- How fast can you arrive?
- Do you work with insurance?
- What happens during the drying process?
- Do you guarantee your work?
- What's included in your free assessment?

## Phase 4: Review Strategy (Week 3 Forward)

Reviews are your strongest trust signal and local ranking factor.

### 4.1 Request Reviews Systematically

After every completed job:

1. Ask directly: "Would you leave us a quick Google review?"
2. Send follow-up text with direct review link
3. Respond to every review within 24 hours

### 4.2 Review Targets

| Timeframe | Target |
|----------|--------|
| Month 1 | 10 reviews |
| Month 3 | 30 reviews |
| Month 6 | 50+ reviews |

### 4.3 Leverage Reviews

- Add star ratings to your site
- Create testimonials page with full reviews

## Phase 6: Social Media & Community (Week 4+)

### 6.1 Facebook Strategy

**Works For:**
- Before/after project photos
- Community engagement
- Seasonal campaigns
- Maintenance plans

**Does NOT Work For:** Emergency calls — people search, not scroll during floods

**Action:**

1. Create Facebook Business Page
2. Post 3–5 times per week:
   - Before/after photos (2x)
   - Educational tips (1x)
   - Community engagement (1x)
   - Testimonials (1x)
3. Enable messaging
4. Add website and phone to page info

### 6.2 Instagram

- Before/after carousels
- Time-lapse project videos
- Reels showing process

### 6.3 Nextdoor

1. Create business presence
2. Answer neighbor questions
3. Share project photos in neighborhoods
4. Run targeted ads by zip code

### 6.4 LinkedIn

- Connect with insurance adjusters
- Share case studies
- Target property managers
